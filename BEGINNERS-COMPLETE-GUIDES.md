## What Makes These Different

These are not cheat sheets or command lists. They are structured learning resources designed for people who are new to a technology:

- **Zero prerequisites** — every guide starts from the very beginning
- **Step-by-step progression** — concepts build on each other logically
- **Practical examples** — working commands and real scenarios throughout
- **Common mistakes covered** — learn what beginners get wrong and how to avoid it
- **Next steps included** — know exactly where to go after completing each guide

## FAQ

**Do I need any prior experience?**
No. Every guide starts from absolute zero. If you can use a computer, you can learn from these.

**How long does it take to complete one guide?**
Each guide is designed for 2-4 hours of focused study. Spread across multiple sessions if needed.

**In what order should I study?**
Follow one of the learning paths above, or start with the technology most relevant to your goal.
