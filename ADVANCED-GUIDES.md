## Who Are These For?

These guides are written for professionals who:

- Have 3+ years of hands-on experience
- Hold senior, staff, or architect-level positions
- Are responsible for systems that must be reliable at scale
- Want to understand the **internals** — not just the API surface
- Are preparing for principal engineer or architect interviews

- **Security hardening** — SELinux policies, certificate authorities, rootless containers
- **Performance engineering** — eBPF tracing, query planner deep dives, buffer pool tuning
- **Custom extensibility** — writing operators, custom providers, plugin development
